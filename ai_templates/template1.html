About Mahadevsthan

The sacred Mahadevsthan is a divine abode where devotees gather every evening to sing bhajans and perform aarti in praise of Lord Shiva. Established in 1950, this temple has been a spiritual center for thousands of devotees seeking blessings, peace, and divine connection.

Daily Schedule

  • Morning Aarti: 6:00 AM
  • Afternoon Puja: 12:00 PM
  • Evening Bhajans: 6:30 PM
  • Night Aarti: 8:00 PM

Temple Features

  • Sacred Shiva Lingam
  • Nandi Bull Statue
  • Bhajan Hall
  • Prasadam Distribution
