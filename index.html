About Mahadevsthan

The sacred Mahadevsthan is a divine abode where devotees gather every evening to sing bhajans and perform aarti in praise of Lord Shiva, Rama, Ganesh, Saraswati and more. This temple has been a spiritual center for thousands of devotees seeking blessings, peace, and divine connection.
